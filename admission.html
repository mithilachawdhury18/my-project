Admission at Brac University

Learn how to apply, important dates, eligibility criteria, tuition fees, and available scholarships.

Undergraduate Admission Requirements

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5 in both SSC and HSC or equivalent
  • IELTS 5.5+ or English Proficiency Test (if applicable)
  • Valid National ID/Birth Certificate and academic transcripts

Important Dates

EventDate
Admission OpenMay 15, 2025
Application DeadlineJune 30, 2025
Admission TestJuly 6, 2025
OrientationAugust 20, 2025

How to Apply

  1. Visit the BracU Admission Portal.
  2. Create an applicant account and fill in the required details.
  3. Upload scanned documents and submit the application fee.
  4. Download admit card and attend admission test/interview.

Tuition & Fees

Undergraduate (per credit): BDT 6,000 – 8,000 depending on department

Other fees: Admission Fee, Lab Fee, Library Fee as applicable

Scholarships & Financial Aid

  • Merit Scholarships (based on GPA or admission test results)
  • Need-based Financial Aid (up to 100%)
  • Freedom Fighter Quota Waiver
  • Special Women in STEM Scholarships
