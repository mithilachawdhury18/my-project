About Brac University

Established in 2001, Brac University (BRACU) is dedicated to providing quality education to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for success. We emphasize excellence in teaching, research, and community service, maintaining the highest standards for all programs. BRACU fosters innovation, leadership, and social responsibility among students to prepare them for global challenges.
