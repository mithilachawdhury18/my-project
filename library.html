Library Overview

The BRAC University Library provides access to thousands of books, e-journals, digital archives, research papers, and academic databases. Our mission is to support learning, teaching, and research through dynamic library services and up-to-date resources.

🖥️ Digital Resources

  • e-Journals: Access to JSTOR, IEEE Xplore, SpringerLink, and more.
  • Digital Archives: Theses, dissertations, and research papers from BRACU students.
  • Online Catalog: Browse books, media, and academic resources online.
  • Remote Access: Use your student ID to log in to digital resources from home.

📝 Membership & Rules

All current students, faculty, and staff are eligible for library membership. Your BRACU ID acts as your library card. Books can be borrowed for 7–14 days depending on the category. Late returns incur a fine of BDT 10 per day.
