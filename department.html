Academic Departments of Brac University

Brac University houses a wide range of academic departments dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

Programs: BSc in CSE, MSc in CSE

Focus Areas: Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Software Engineering, Cybersecurity

Facilities: AI Lab, Robotics Lab, Software Dev Lab

Website: cse.bracu.ac.bd

Electrical & Electronic Engineering (EEE)

Programs: BSc in EEE, MSc in EEE

Focus Areas: Power Systems, Embedded Systems, Signal Processing, IoT

Labs: Power Electronics, Control Systems, Microcontroller

Website: eee.bracu.ac.bd

School of Business

Programs: BBA, MBA, Executive MBA

Specializations: Finance, Marketing, HRM, Entrepreneurship

Key Features: International case studies, internships, startup incubation

Website: Business School

Architecture

Programs: Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch)

Focus Areas: Sustainable Design, Urban Planning, Landscape Architecture

Studio Environment: Collaborative and project-based learning

Website: arch.bracu.ac.bd

Pharmacy

Programs: Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

Areas of Study: Pharmacology, Clinical Pharmacy, Pharmaceutical Technology

Facilities: Modern labs, hospital partnerships, research opportunities

Website: pharmacy.bracu.ac.bd

English & Humanities

Programs: BA in English, MA in ELT

Focus: Literature, Linguistics, Creative Writing, Postcolonial Studies

Strengths: Experienced faculty, interactive seminars, writing workshops

Website: eh.bracu.ac.bd
