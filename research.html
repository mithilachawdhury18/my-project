Research & Innovation

Overview

BRAC University emphasizes impactful, interdisciplinary research that addresses real-world problems. Our faculty and students collaborate across departments to lead innovation in science, technology, social development, and sustainability.

Key Research Areas

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Exploring deep learning, natural language processing, and automation to solve complex problems in health, education, and industry.

Climate Change & Sustainability

Developing sustainable solutions to environmental issues, disaster resilience, and energy efficiency through research and policy.

Public Health & Epidemiology

Collaborative research on healthcare systems, disease prevention, mental health, and global health security.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Fostering startup culture and creating innovative tech-based solutions through incubators and business research.
