Overview

BRAC University provides well-organized and efficient transport services for students, faculty, and staff. Our bus fleet ensures timely and safe travel between the campus and various parts of Dhaka city.

Key Features

  • Air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned buses
  • Tracking system for bus schedules and routes
  • Experienced and professional drivers
  • Dedicated student and faculty transport
  • Morning and evening shuttle services

Routes & Stops

Transport services cover major locations like Uttara, Mirpur, Gulshan, Dhanmondi, Mohammadpur, and Banasree. Bus stops are designated to ensure maximum accessibility and convenience.

Safety & Comfort

All buses undergo routine maintenance and safety checks. Emergency contact and first aid support are available onboard. Female students are given priority seating.
